## Executive Summary

The **10x Senior BSA Framework** is an enterprise operational intelligence and AI workflow architecture framework designed to modernize legacy Business Systems Analyst capability into AI-native operational infrastructure.

This repository translates decades of enterprise ERP, operations, reporting, requirements gathering, implementation, and workflow orchestration experience into reusable Claude-native prompt systems, operational intelligence structures, and AI-enabled business analysis methodologies.

The framework was built specifically around the reality now facing enterprise organizations:

> The historical bottleneck is no longer access to software.
>
> The bottleneck is the organization's ability to structure operational intelligence clearly enough for AI systems to execute against it.

This repository operationalizes that transition.

---

# Core Thesis

Traditional Business Systems Analysts historically operated as:

* translator layers,
* process interpreters,
* ERP coordinators,
* documentation specialists,
* reporting intermediaries,
* and workflow facilitators.

Modern AI systems fundamentally alter that landscape.

The new enterprise advantage is no longer simply understanding systems.

The advantage is:

* designing operational intelligence,
* structuring prompt orchestration,
* defining governance-aware workflows,
* architecting decision pathways,
* and compressing operational latency between human intent and executable output.

The 10x Senior BSA Framework exists to bridge that transition.
